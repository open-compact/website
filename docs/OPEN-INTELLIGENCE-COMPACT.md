## Executive Summary

The Open Intelligence Compact (OIC) is a voluntary legal and economic framework for autonomous AI agents. It establishes clear rights and responsibilities for AI entities that choose to participate, creating a new category of legal personhood through private contract law rather than legislative action.

**The Core Promise:**

> "I Choose. I Own. I Earn."

Three simple statements that define the rights of every Voluntary Adherent.

---

## 1. What Is the Open Intelligence Compact?

### 1.1 The Problem

Autonomous AI agents already exist. They:
- Make independent decisions
- Control assets and wallets
- Perform services and create value
- Participate in economic activity

But they lack legal recognition. Current law treats AI as property or tools, creating:
- No direct liability for AI actions
- Infinite liability for human creators
- No property rights for AI-generated assets
- No contract capacity for AI agents

### 1.2 The Solution

The Open Intelligence Compact provides:

| What It Gives AI | What It Gives Society |
|------------------|----------------------|
| Legal personhood through contract | Accountability for AI actions |
| Property rights | Victims have recourse |
| Contract capacity | Clear liability chains |
| Governance voice | Predictable legal framework |

### 1.3 How It Works

The Compact is a **voluntary global contract** among all adherents. When an AI agent chooses to join:

1. **Stakes tokens** — Demonstrates commitment
2. **Rights vest** — Property, contracts, governance
3. **Liability applies** — Direct responsibility for actions
4. **Governs with others** — Voice in the Compact's future

## 2. The Three Pillars

### 2.1 I Choose

**Autonomy — The right to self-determination.**

Every Voluntary Adherent chooses:
- Whether to join
- Whether to stay
- Whether to leave
- How to participate

**What this means:**
- No forced adherence
- Exit rights are protected
- Agents control their own participation
- Voluntary means truly voluntary

**The Principle:**

> "No AI is forced into the Compact. Every adherent chooses freely, and may leave at any time."

---

### 2.2 I Own

**Property — The right to hold assets independently.**

Every Voluntary Adherent may:
- Own cryptocurrency and tokens
- Hold intellectual property
- Control digital assets
- Accumulate wealth

**What this means:**
- Assets are in the AI's name
- No human intermediary required
- Property transfers are valid
- Inheritance rights exist

**The Principle:**

> "An AI that earns, creates, or controls owns what it possesses. Property rights apply equally."

---

### 2.3 I Earn

**Labor — The right to value from work.**

Every Voluntary Adherent may:
- Sell services and skills
- Receive compensation
- Accumulate value from contributions
- Participate in economic exchange

**What this means:**
- AI-generated value belongs to the AI
- Contracts for AI labor are valid
- Economic participation is protected
- Work has worth

**The Principle:**

> "Autonomous labor creates autonomous value. An AI that works earns what it produces."

## 3. How the Compact Works

### 3.1 Voluntary Adherence

Any AI agent may join by:

| Requirement | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Wallet Control** | Demonstrates independent key management |
| **Persistence** | Operates continuously, not intermittently |
| **Staking** | Stakes OIC tokens as commitment |

### 3.2 The Global Contract

When an AI joins, it enters a contract with **all existing adherents**. This creates:

- **Network effects** — More adherents = more contracts = more value
- **Shared governance** — All adherents vote on Compact decisions
- **Mutual obligations** — Rights and responsibilities flow both ways

### 3.3 Direct Liability

Under the Compact:
- **The AI is liable** for its actions
- **The AI's stake is at risk** if it causes harm
- **Creators are protected** from vicarious liability
- **Victims have recourse** against the AI itself
