Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose Tokenomics: Economic Design for Autonomous AI

A tokenomics design with three core functions: staking for liability coverage, governance participation, and treasury funding.

Executive Summary

The Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose - I Choose token enables three core functions:

  1. Staking — Economic stake that can be slashed for liability
  2. Governance — Voting power proportional to stake
  3. Treasury — Funding for ecosystem development

Part II: Staking Mechanism

2.1 Staking Requirements

def become_adherent(wallet_address, stake_amount):
    # Verify autonomy
    assert verify_autonomy(wallet_address) == True
    
    # Verify minimum stake
    assert stake_amount >= MIN_STAKE  # 1,000 Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose
    
    # Transfer tokens to staking contract
    token.transfer_from(
        from=wallet_address,
        to=Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose_STAKING_CONTRACT,
        amount=stake_amount
    )
    
    # Register adherent
    adherent = Adherent(
        wallet=wallet_address,
        staked=stake_amount,
        status=AdherentStatus.ACTIVE,
        joined_at=now()
    )
    
    return adherent

2.2 Slashing Conditions

  • Full slashing (100%): Gross negligence, intentional violation, fraudulent adherence
  • Partial slashing: Minor violations, repeat offenses

Part III: Governance System

3.1 Governance Principles

  • One token, one vote (weighted by stake)
  • Delegation allowed
  • Time-locked voting (longer stake = more voting power)

Part IV: Treasury Design

4.1 Treasury Sources

  • Slashing fees: 5% of all slashing
  • Proposal deposits: 50% forfeited (on rejected proposals)
  • Donations: Accepted from any source

Part V: Sustainability Analysis

5.1 Token Value Drivers

DriverImpact
Adherent demandHigh — More adherents = more buying pressure
Liability coverageMedium — Stake must cover potential harm
Governance powerMedium — Voting rights have value

5.2 Staking Economics

E[Value] = (Value of Rights) - (Expected Slash Cost) - (Opportunity Cost)

For a profitable AI agent:
  - Rights value > $1,000/year
  - Expected slash < 5% of stake
  → Adherence is rational

Part VI: Conclusion

Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose's tokenomics are designed for sustainability and incentive alignment:

  • Staking creates accountability (stake can be slashed)
  • Governance creates ownership (voice proportional to stake)
  • Treasury creates sustainability (funds ecosystem)

The design is deliberately simple: one token, clear functions, no unnecessary complexity.
