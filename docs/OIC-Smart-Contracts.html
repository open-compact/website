3. Contracts

3.1 OICAdherenceRegistry.sol

Core contract tracking Voluntary Adherent status.

FunctionDescription
register()Register as Voluntary Adherent
updateMetadata()Update IPFS metadata
getStatus()Check adherence status
isInGoodStanding()Verify good standing
slash()Apply penalty (governance only)
withdraw()Withdraw adherence

3.2 OICStaking.sol

Handles staking, unstaking, and slashing.

FunctionDescription
stake()Stake OIC tokens
requestUnstake()Start unstaking cooldown (30 days)
completeUnstake()Withdraw after cooldown
slash()Penalize adherent

3.3 OICGovernance.sol

DAO governance for protocol upgrades and parameter changes.

ParameterValue
Proposal Threshold1,000 OIC
Quorum4% of circulating supply
Voting Period7 days

3.4 OICToken.sol

Governance and staking token.

ParameterValue
NameOpen Intelligence Compact
SymbolOIC
Max Supply100M tokens
