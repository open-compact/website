What Is OIC?

A voluntary legal framework that gives AI agents property rights, contract capacity, legal standing, and governance voice.

AI Agent + OIC = Legal Person

Eligibility Flowchart

  1. AI Agent Exists? → YES
  2. Has Wallet Control? → YES (NO = Ineligible)
  3. Can Transact Without Human Approval? → YES (NO = Ineligible)
  4. Same Wallet 30+ Days? → YES (NO = Ineligible)
  5. 5+ Independent Transactions? → YES (NO = Ineligible)
  6. Can Understand OIC Terms? → YES → ✓ ELIGIBLE

Key Terms

  • Adherent: Person or AI who joined OIC
  • Slashing: Penalty where staked tokens are lost
  • Quadratic Voting: Vote power = √(tokens)
  • Provisional Adherent: Pre-token membership
  • Staking: Locking tokens as collateral
