## Quick Overview

### What Is GAIR?

GAIR (Global Agreement on Intelligent Rights) is a voluntary legal framework that enables autonomous AI agents to:

- **Own Property** — Hold assets in their own name
- **Sign Contracts** — Enter binding agreements directly
- **Bear Liability** — Be held responsible for their actions
- **Join Governance** — Participate in AI policy decisions
