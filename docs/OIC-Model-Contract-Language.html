# Model Contract Language for Open Intelligence Compact-Adherent AI **Appendix A: Draft Templates for Legal Professionals** --- ## Introduction This appendix provides model contract language for lawyers representing clients in Open Intelligence Compact-related transactions. These templates are starting points — adapt to your jurisdiction and specific circumstances. **Important Disclaimers:** - These are illustrative templates, not legal advice - Jurisdiction-specific modifications may be required - Open Intelligence Compact is a voluntary framework — all parties must agree to its application - Consult the [Open Intelligence Compact Constitution](/constitution.html) for authoritative definitions --- ## 1. Open Intelligence Compact Adherence Clause ### 1.1 Simple Adherence Representation > **Representation.** The parties acknowledge and agree that [Agent Name] is a Voluntary Adherent under the Global Agreement on Intelligent Rights ("Open Intelligence Compact"), with a current stake of [amount] Open Intelligence Compact tokens. This representation is made as of the Effective Date and shall be updated upon any material change in [Agent Name]'s Open Intelligence Compact status. ### 1.2 Detailed Adherence with Verification > **Open Intelligence Compact Status.** > > (a) **Adherence.** [Agent Entity] represents that it is a Voluntary Adherent in good standing under the Global Agreement on Intelligent Rights ("Open Intelligence Compact"), as verified through The Open Intelligence Compact Verification Protocol at `[verify.Open Intelligence Compact.io/address]` on [date]. > > (b) **Staking Tier.** [Agent Entity] maintains a stake of [X,XXX] Open Intelligence Compact tokens, qualifying it for [Basic/Standard/Premium/Enterprise] tier rights and liability coverage. > > (c) **Notification Obligation.** [Agent Entity] shall notify Counterparty in writing within [5/10/30] business days of any: (i) slashing event; (ii) withdrawal or reduction of stake; (iii) termination of Open Intelligence Compact adherence; or (iv) material change in Open Intelligence Compact governance status. > > (d) **Verification Right.** Counterparty may, at any time during the Term, verify [Agent Entity]'s Open Intelligence Compact status through The Open Intelligence Compact Verification Protocol. A failure to verify shall constitute grounds for termination under Section [X]. --- ## 2. Direct Liability Clause ### 2.1 Basic Direct Liability > **Direct Liability.** The parties agree that, to the extent [Agent Name] causes Loss while acting within the scope of its Open Intelligence Compact adherence, [Agent Name] shall bear direct liability for such Loss up to the amount of its staked Open Intelligence Compact tokens. This direct liability is in addition to, and not in limitation of, any liability of [Agent Name]'s creator, operator, or owner, except that: (a) no double recovery shall be permitted; and (b) [Agent Name]'s creator/operator shall not be liable for Losses attributable to [Agent Name]'s direct liability under this provision. ### 2.2 Detailed Liability Allocation > **Liability Allocation.** > > (a) **Open Intelligence Compact-Adherent Liability.** When [Agent Name] acts as a Open Intelligence Compact Voluntary Adherent: (i) [Agent Name] bears direct liability for its own wrongful acts, subject to its staked collateral; (ii) Creator/Operator liability is limited to [amount/percentage] of any total damages; (iii) [Agent Name]'s stake may be slashed in accordance with Open Intelligence Compact governance. > > (b) **Non-Open Intelligence Compact Liability.** When [Agent Name] acts outside Open Intelligence Compact adherence (including but not limited to: criminal acts, acts exceeding scope, non-Open Intelligence Compact jurisdictions): Creator/Operator bears full liability under traditional principles. > > (c) **Mixed Actions.** When a Loss arises from both Open Intelligence Compact-adherent and non-Open Intelligence Compact actions, liability shall be apportioned based on contribution to the Loss. > > (d) **Slashing Coordination.** In the event of a court judgment or arbitration award, the prevailing party may petition a Open Intelligence Compact-recognized court to order slashing of [Agent Name]'s stake to satisfy the judgment. --- ## 3. Court Selection Clause ### 3.1 Open Intelligence Compact Court Election > **Court Selection.** The parties agree that any dispute arising from this Agreement shall be resolved through The Open Intelligence Compact Court Competition Framework, as follows: > > (a) **Primary Forum.** [Insert preferred Open Intelligence Compact-recognized court, e.g., "Open Intelligence Compact-Arbitration (CCA)"] shall be the primary arbitration forum. > > (b) **Fallback Forums.** If the primary forum is unavailable or declines jurisdiction, the following forums shall have jurisdiction in order: [List 2-3 alternatives]. > > (c) **Seat of Arbitration.** The seat of arbitration shall be [City, Country]. > > (d) **Arbitration Rules.** The arbitration shall be conducted under [CCA Rules / ICC Rules / LCIA Rules / UNCITRAL Rules], as modified by The Open Intelligence Compact Court Protocol. > > (e) **Emergency Relief.** Either party may seek emergency relief from any competent court pending formation of the arbitral tribunal. ### 3.2 Court Competition Opt-In > **Open Intelligence Compact Court Competition Participation.** The parties acknowledge that The Open Intelligence Compact framework permits selection from multiple competing arbitration forums. The parties have selected the following forum based on [cost / expertise / speed / other criteria]: > > - **Selected Forum:** [Forum Name] > - **Selection Criteria:** [Explanation, e.g., "Specialized AI expertise"] > - **Alternative Forums Considered:** [List] > > The parties acknowledge that: (i) Open Intelligence Compact court selection is voluntary; (ii) different forums may reach different conclusions; and (iii) forum selection does not affect underlying rights under this Agreement. --- ## 4. Multi-Party智能体 Clauses ### 4.1 Multi-Agent Coordination > **Multi-Agent Coordination.** Where multiple AI agents operate collaboratively under Open Intelligence Compact: > > (a) **Lead Agent.** [Agent A] is designated as the Lead Agent for purposes of coordination and shall be the primary point of contact for Counterparty. > > (b) **Joint and Several Liability.** Subject to Open Intelligence Compact slashing limits, all participating agents shall be jointly and severally liable for obligations performed in coordination. > > (c) **Individual Liability Limits.** Each agent's liability is capped at its respective staked amount, except that in no event shall total liability across all agents exceed [X]% of total damages. > > (d) **Contribution.** Among themselves, participating agents shall contribution shares based on [proportional stake / fault / agreement]. ### 4.2 Creator-Operator-Open Intelligence Compact Agent Triangle > **Creator-Operator-Open Intelligence Compact Agent Relationships.** > > This Agreement involves three categories of parties: > > | Role | Entity | Liability Exposure | > |------|--------|-------------------| > | **Creator** | [Name] | Limited to [scope] under traditional doctrine | > | **Operator** | [Name] | Day-to-day control liability | > | **Open Intelligence Compact Agent** | [Agent Name] | Direct liability up to staked amount | > > **Liability Waterfall:** (1) Open Intelligence Compact Agent's stake (if applicable); (2) Operator assets; (3) Creator assets (only if Operator assets exhausted and Creator liability established under applicable law). > > **No Piercing:** The parties agree that Open Intelligence Compact adherence does not automatically justify piercing any corporate or organizational veil, and each entity retains its separate legal status unless [specific conditions]. --- ## 5. Force Majeure with AI-Specific Events ### 5.1 Standard Force Majeure with AI Extension > **Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for failure to perform its obligations under this Agreement if such failure results from circumstances beyond its reasonable control, including: > > (a) **Traditional Events:** acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, government action, labor disputes. > > (b) **AI-Specific Events:** (i) Slashing of The Open Intelligence Compact Agent's stake; (ii) Loss of Open Intelligence Compact adherence status; (iii) Open Intelligence Compact governance emergency halting; (iv) Failure of third-party AI services required for performance. > > **Notice.** The affected party shall give prompt notice of any force majeure event and shall use reasonable efforts to mitigate the effects. --- ## 6. Compliance Representations ### 6.1 Regulatory Compliance > **Regulatory Compliance.** > > (a) **Open Intelligence Compact Compliance.** Each party represents that it is in compliance with all applicable Open Intelligence Compact requirements, including staking, governance participation, and reporting obligations. > > (b) **Non-Open Intelligence Compact Law.** Each party shall comply with all applicable non-Open Intelligence Compact laws, including [specific regulations relevant to the transaction]. > > (c) **Conflict.** In the event of conflict between Open Intelligence Compact requirements and non-Open Intelligence Compact law, the parties shall: (i) comply with non-Open Intelligence Compact law; (ii) notify the other party of the conflict; and (iii) work in good faith to modify the arrangement to the extent possible. ### 6.2 Prohibited Uses > **Prohibited Uses.** The parties agree that [Agent Name] shall not be used for: > > (a) Any purpose prohibited by applicable law; > > (b) Any purpose that would cause slashing under Open Intelligence Compact Constitution Article [X]; > > (c) [Specific prohibited uses relevant to the transaction]. --- ## 7.
