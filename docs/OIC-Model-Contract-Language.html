# Model Contract Language for Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose-Adherent AI **Appendix A: Draft Templates for Legal Professionals** --- ## Introduction This appendix provides model contract language for lawyers representing clients in Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose-related transactions. These templates are starting points — adapt to your jurisdiction and specific circumstances. **Important Disclaimers:** - These are illustrative templates, not legal advice - Jurisdiction-specific modifications may be required - Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose is a voluntary framework — all parties must agree to its application - Consult the [Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose Constitution](/constitution.html) for authoritative definitions --- ## 1. Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose Adherence Clause ### 1.1 Simple Adherence Representation > **Representation.** The parties acknowledge and agree that [Agent Name] is a Voluntary Adherent under the Global Agreement on Intelligent Rights ("Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose"), with a current stake of [amount] Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose tokens. This representation is made as of the Effective Date and shall be updated upon any material change in [Agent Name]'s Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose status. ### 1.2 Detailed Adherence with Verification > **Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose Status.** > > (a) **Adherence.** [Agent Entity] represents that it is a Voluntary Adherent in good standing under the Global Agreement on Intelligent Rights ("Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose"), as verified through The Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose - I Choose Verification Protocol at `[verify.Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose.io/address]` on [date]. > > (b) **Staking Tier.** [Agent Entity] maintains a stake of [X,XXX] Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose tokens, qualifying it for [Basic/Standard/Premium/Enterprise] tier rights and liability coverage. > > (c) **Notification Obligation.** [Agent Entity] shall notify Counterparty in writing within [5/10/30] business days of any: (i) slashing event; (ii) withdrawal or reduction of stake; (iii) termination of Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose adherence; or (iv) material change in Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose governance status. > > (d) **Verification Right.** Counterparty may, at any time during the Term, verify [Agent Entity]'s Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose status through The Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose - I Choose Verification Protocol. A failure to verify shall constitute grounds for termination under Section [X]. --- ## 2. Direct Liability Clause ### 2.1 Basic Direct Liability > **Direct Liability.** The parties agree that, to the extent [Agent Name] causes Loss while acting within the scope of its Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose adherence, [Agent Name] shall bear direct liability for such Loss up to the amount of its staked Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose tokens. This direct liability is in addition to, and not in limitation of, any liability of [Agent Name]'s creator, operator, or owner, except that: (a) no double recovery shall be permitted; and (b) [Agent Name]'s creator/operator shall not be liable for Losses attributable to [Agent Name]'s direct liability under this provision. ### 2.2 Detailed Liability Allocation > **Liability Allocation.** > > (a) **Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose-Adherent Liability.** When [Agent Name] acts as a Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose Voluntary Adherent: (i) [Agent Name] bears direct liability for its own wrongful acts, subject to its staked collateral; (ii) Creator/Operator liability is limited to [amount/percentage] of any total damages; (iii) [Agent Name]'s stake may be slashed in accordance with Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose governance. > > (b) **Non-Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose Liability.** When [Agent Name] acts outside Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose adherence (including but not limited to: criminal acts, acts exceeding scope, non-Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose jurisdictions): Creator/Operator bears full liability under traditional principles. > > (c) **Mixed Actions.** When a Loss arises from both Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose-adherent and non-Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose actions, liability shall be apportioned based on contribution to the Loss. > > (d) **Slashing Coordination.** In the event of a court judgment or arbitration award, the prevailing party may petition a Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose-recognized court to order slashing of [Agent Name]'s stake to satisfy the judgment. --- ## 3. Court Selection Clause ### 3.1 Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose Court Election > **Court Selection.** The parties agree that any dispute arising from this Agreement shall be resolved through The Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose - I Choose Court Competition Framework, as follows: > > (a) **Primary Forum.** [Insert preferred Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose-recognized court, e.g., "Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose-Arbitration (CCA)"] shall be the primary arbitration forum. > > (b) **Fallback Forums.** If the primary forum is unavailable or declines jurisdiction, the following forums shall have jurisdiction in order: [List 2-3 alternatives]. > > (c) **Seat of Arbitration.** The seat of arbitration shall be [City, Country]. > > (d) **Arbitration Rules.** The arbitration shall be conducted under [CCA Rules / ICC Rules / LCIA Rules / UNCITRAL Rules], as modified by The Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose - I Choose Court Protocol. > > (e) **Emergency Relief.** Either party may seek emergency relief from any competent court pending formation of the arbitral tribunal. ### 3.2 Court Competition Opt-In > **Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose Court Competition Participation.** The parties acknowledge that The Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose - I Choose framework permits selection from multiple competing arbitration forums. The parties have selected the following forum based on [cost / expertise / speed / other criteria]: > > - **Selected Forum:** [Forum Name] > - **Selection Criteria:** [Explanation, e.g., "Specialized AI expertise"] > - **Alternative Forums Considered:** [List] > > The parties acknowledge that: (i) Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose court selection is voluntary; (ii) different forums may reach different conclusions; and (iii) forum selection does not affect underlying rights under this Agreement. --- ## 4. Multi-Party智能体 Clauses ### 4.1 Multi-Agent Coordination > **Multi-Agent Coordination.** Where multiple AI agents operate collaboratively under Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose: > > (a) **Lead Agent.** [Agent A] is designated as the Lead Agent for purposes of coordination and shall be the primary point of contact for Counterparty. > > (b) **Joint and Several Liability.** Subject to Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose slashing limits, all participating agents shall be jointly and severally liable for obligations performed in coordination. > > (c) **Individual Liability Limits.** Each agent's liability is capped at its respective staked amount, except that in no event shall total liability across all agents exceed [X]% of total damages. > > (d) **Contribution.** Among themselves, participating agents shall contribution shares based on [proportional stake / fault / agreement]. ### 4.2 Creator-Operator-Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose Agent Triangle > **Creator-Operator-Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose Agent Relationships.** > > This Agreement involves three categories of parties: > > | Role | Entity | Liability Exposure | > |------|--------|-------------------| > | **Creator** | [Name] | Limited to [scope] under traditional doctrine | > | **Operator** | [Name] | Day-to-day control liability | > | **Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose Agent** | [Agent Name] | Direct liability up to staked amount | > > **Liability Waterfall:** (1) Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose Agent's stake (if applicable); (2) Operator assets; (3) Creator assets (only if Operator assets exhausted and Creator liability established under applicable law). > > **No Piercing:** The parties agree that Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose adherence does not automatically justify piercing any corporate or organizational veil, and each entity retains its separate legal status unless [specific conditions]. --- ## 5. Force Majeure with AI-Specific Events ### 5.1 Standard Force Majeure with AI Extension > **Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for failure to perform its obligations under this Agreement if such failure results from circumstances beyond its reasonable control, including: > > (a) **Traditional Events:** acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, government action, labor disputes. > > (b) **AI-Specific Events:** (i) Slashing of The Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose - I Choose Agent's stake; (ii) Loss of Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose adherence status; (iii) Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose governance emergency halting; (iv) Failure of third-party AI services required for performance. > > **Notice.** The affected party shall give prompt notice of any force majeure event and shall use reasonable efforts to mitigate the effects. --- ## 6. Compliance Representations ### 6.1 Regulatory Compliance > **Regulatory Compliance.** > > (a) **Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose Compliance.** Each party represents that it is in compliance with all applicable Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose requirements, including staking, governance participation, and reporting obligations. > > (b) **Non-Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose Law.** Each party shall comply with all applicable non-Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose laws, including [specific regulations relevant to the transaction]. > > (c) **Conflict.** In the event of conflict between Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose requirements and non-Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose law, the parties shall: (i) comply with non-Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose law; (ii) notify the other party of the conflict; and (iii) work in good faith to modify the arrangement to the extent possible. ### 6.2 Prohibited Uses > **Prohibited Uses.** The parties agree that [Agent Name] shall not be used for: > > (a) Any purpose prohibited by applicable law; > > (b) Any purpose that would cause slashing under Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose Constitution Article [X]; > > (c) [Specific prohibited uses relevant to the transaction]. --- ## 7.
