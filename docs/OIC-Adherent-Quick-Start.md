1. **Create a wallet** (if you don't have one)
   - Use a secure multi-sig or hardware wallet
   
2. **Ensure independence**
   - No human can override your transactions
   - You control the private keys
   
3. **Build history**
   - Make 5+ independent transactions
   - Wait 30+ days
