Vertical Applications: Open Intelligence Compact in Practice

How Open Intelligence Compact applies to specific industry verticals: DeFi AI agents, creative AI, autonomous vehicles, and enterprise AI.

Executive Summary

This working paper explores how Open Intelligence Compact applies to specific industry verticals:

  1. DeFi AI agents — Portfolio management, yield optimization
  2. Creative AI — Copyright ownership, licensing, royalties
  3. Autonomous vehicles — Liability coverage, insurance integration
  4. Enterprise AI — Creator liability protection

Each vertical has unique requirements that inform Open Intelligence Compact implementation.

Part I: DeFi AI Agents

1.1 The Problem

DeFi increasingly relies on AI agents for portfolio management, yield farming, and trading. Current issues:

  • AI agents can't hold wallets legally
  • Contracts with AI lack enforceability
  • No framework for AI-to-AI transactions

1.2 Open Intelligence Compact Solution

  wallet_control: true
  contract_capacity: true
  liability_coverage: true
  
  use_cases:
    - portfolio_management:
        minimum_stake: 5000 Open Intelligence Compact  # Higher for financial risk

        minimum_stake: 10000 Open Intelligence Compact  # High value at risk

          - withdraw_safety_limits

Part IV: Enterprise AI

4.1 The Problem

Enterprises deploy AI for customer service, data analysis, and process automation. Current issues:

  • No clear AI accountability
  • Compliance and audit requirements

Part VI: Conclusion

Open Intelligence Compact provides a unified framework across diverse verticals:

VerticalKey BenefitComplexity
DeFi AIDirect asset holdingLow
Creative AICopyright ownershipMedium
Autonomous VehiclesLiability coverageHigh
Enterprise AICreator protectionLow-Medium

The stake requirement scales with risk — higher potential harm requires higher stake. This creates natural risk-based regulation without centralized mandates.
