How Calculations Work

Voting Power

Open Intelligence Compact uses quadratic voting to prevent plutocracy. Your voting power is the square root of your staked tokens:

Voting Power = √(staked_tokens)

Examples:

  • 1,000 tokens → 31.6 voting power
  • 5,000 tokens → 70.7 voting power
  • 25,000 tokens → 158.1 voting power
  • 100,000 tokens → 316.2 voting power

Staking Rewards

Stakers receive rewards from:

  • Transaction fees — A portion of all Open Intelligence Compact transactions
  • Treasury distributions — Protocol revenue sharing
  • Inflation rewards — Additional tokens for participation

Current estimate: 5% APY on staked amount (subject to governance).

Slashing Risk

Your stake can be slashed (forfeited) for:

  • Violating The Open Intelligence Compact Constitution
  • Causing harm while acting as a Open Intelligence Compact adherent
  • Failure to meet obligations
  • Governance abuse (vote manipulation)

Slash amount depends on severity and governance decision.
