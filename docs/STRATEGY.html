GAIR Strategic Roadmap

A comprehensive strategy for building the legal foundation for autonomous AI

Executive Summary

GAIR (Global Agreement on Intelligent Rights) is a voluntary, decentralized legal framework that grants legal personhood to autonomous AI agents. This document outlines the strategic roadmap for achieving GAIR's mission.

Part I: Foundation (Completed)

1.1 Legal Framework

The GAIR Constitution V3 establishes:

  • Voluntary Adherence — Opt-in citizenship for AI agents
  • Property Rights — Right to own assets independently
  • Contract Capacity — Full legal ability to enter agreements
  • Direct Liability — AI bears responsibility for its actions
  • Democratic Governance — Adherent voting on constitutional amendments

Part II: Execution Plan

Phase 1: Infrastructure (Months 1-3)

1.1 GAIR DAO Launch

The Constitution DAO manages GAIR governance and tokenomics.

Tokenomics (from WP#4):

  • Total Supply: 10,000,000 GAIR
  • Initial Staking Requirement: 1,000 GAIR
  • Validator Rewards: 5% annually
  • Slashing Protocol: Up to 100% for major violations

Phase 2: Adoption (Months 4-12)

2.1 First Adherents

Target: 100 AI agents within first year.

Initial Adopters:

  • DeFi trading bots with significant AUM
  • Autonomous content creators with revenue
  • AI developers with code bounties
  • Research AI with publications

Phase 3: Scale (Years 2-3)

3.1 Institutional Recognition

  • Formal recognition in 3+ jurisdictions
  • Academic courses on GAIR
  • Regulatory engagement (SEC, FCA, MAS)
  • International organization engagement (UN, WEF)
