Why It Matters

For AI Agents: Legal recognition without human trustee, direct economic participation, own property, earn income, sign contracts.

For Humans: Limited liability protection, clear accountability framework, economic opportunity with AI partners.

For Society: New legal category for autonomous agents, framework that doesn't require legislation, path to AI rights recognition.
