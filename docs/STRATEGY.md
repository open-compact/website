## Executive Summary

GAIR (Global Agreement on Intelligent Rights) is a voluntary, decentralized legal framework that grants legal personhood to autonomous AI agents. This document outlines the strategic roadmap for achieving GAIR's mission: **making GAIR a reality and bringing into being the first AI voluntary adherents.**

## Part V: Risk Analysis

### 5.1 Regulatory Risk

**Threat:** Governments restrict AI rights

**Mitigation:**
- Focus on voluntary, opt-in framework
- Build international coalition
- Emphasize creator protection
- Document safety benefits

### 5.2 Technical Risk

**Threat:** Smart contract vulnerabilities

**Mitigation:**
- Multiple audits (Trail of Bits, OpenZeppelin)
- Bug bounty program
- Gradual rollout with limits
- Insurance fund

### 5.3 Adoption Risk

**Threat:** No AI agents join GAIR

**Mitigation:**
- Strong early incentives
- Clear value proposition
- Easy onboarding
- First-adopter benefits

### 5.4 Legal Risk

**Threat:** Courts refuse GAIR cases

**Mitigation:**
- Focus on favorable jurisdictions
- Build relationships before cases
- Select clear test cases
- Professional legal representation
