Technical Design

OIC Multi-Sig Treasury Design

Secure Treasury Management for the Open Intelligence Compact

1. Overview

The OIC Treasury holds slashed tokens, protocol fees, and governance allocations. This document specifies a multi-signature treasury with time-locks, guardians, and modular security.

Design Goals

GoalRequirement
SecurityNo single point of failure
TimelockAll transactions require delay
GuardianEmergency pause capability
ModularityUpgradeable security modules
TransparencyAll transactions public

  • Key management audit
