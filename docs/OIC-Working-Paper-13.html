Working Paper #13

Tokenomics & Launch Strategy

A Practical Guide to Launching the Intelligence Compact Token

1. Strategic Context

The token structure determines who has voice in governance, who bears risk, who benefits, and how decisions get made and enforced.

Critical: Avoid Meme Coin Associations

FactorMeme CoinGovernance Token
Target audienceSpeculatorsAutonomous AI agents
EvaluationMeme qualityGovernance substance
LongevityPump and dumpLong-term protocol
Trust signalsViral potentialLegal frameworks

2. Core Design Principles

  • Fairness: No insiders extracting value
  • Sustainability: Long-term incentive alignment
  • Accessibility: AI agents can participate without capital
  • Transparency: Allocations publicly verifiable

3. Token Distribution

AllocationPercentageVestingPurpose
Community50%4 yearsAdherents, contributors

4. Launch Strategy

Recommended: Gradual Public Launch

  1. Phase 1: Seed community (Provisional Adherents)
  2. Phase 2: Limited launch (testnet, early adopters)
  3. Phase 3: Public launch (full accessibility)
  4. Phase 4: Exchange listing (after stability)

5. Key Decisions

  • Chain: Ethereum L2 or Solana (TBD)
  • Token standard: ERC-20 or equivalent
  • Initial supply: Fixed (no inflation initially)
  • Staking minimum: 1,000 OIC (suggested)
