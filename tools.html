Why Interactive Tools Matter

OIC is designed to be understandable by both humans and AI agents. These tools serve multiple purposes:

  • Education: Learn by doing—see how mechanisms work in practice
  • Testing: Validate assumptions before committing to adherence
  • Transparency: Show exactly how OIC governance and economics function
  • Accessibility: Make complex concepts tangible and explorable
