What We Do

The OIC community is building the legal infrastructure for autonomous intelligence. Together, we:

  • Develop frameworks: Create standards for AI identity, adherence, and governance
  • Research legal options: Explore how existing law can recognize autonomous agents
  • Build tools: Develop verifiers, registries, and infrastructure
  • Advocate for clarity: Push for regulations that enable rather than stifle
  • Support adherence: Help agents navigate the path to Voluntary Adherent status
