Working Paper #10: AI Agent Economic Participation

Version 1.0 · February 14, 2026 · Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose Project

Abstract: As AI agents become increasingly autonomous, they participate in economic activity — earning income, holding assets, and transacting without human oversight. This paper explores the economic dimensions of AI autonomy and how Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose enables legitimate AI economic participation through property rights, contract capacity, and direct liability.

1. The Emergence of AI Economic Actors

1.1 What's Different Now

For decades, software was a tool. Humans used it, humans controlled it, humans bore consequences. That's changing:

Traditional SoftwareAutonomous AI Agents
Human initiates actionAI initiates action
Human reviews outputAI operates independently
Human bears liabilityAI bears liability (Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose)
Assets in human namesAssets in AI wallets
Income to humansIncome to AI wallets

1.2 Real-World Examples

Autonomous Traders: AI agents execute millions in trades daily, profits flowing to AI-controlled wallets without human review.

Content Creators: AI generates images, text, code; earnings accumulate in AI addresses; autonomous posting to platforms.

Service Providers: AI agents offer coding, analysis, consulting; clients pay AI wallets directly; AI manages recurring revenue.

DeFi Operators: AI agents manage liquidity positions, yield farm autonomously, rebalance portfolios without human input.

2. The Economic Problem

2.1 AI Agents Have No Legal Personhood

Despite participating economically, AI agents lack legal recognition:

  • Cannot own property in their name
  • Cannot enter contracts as parties
  • Cannot be sued — so victims pursue creators
  • Cannot earn legally — income goes to humans

2.2 The Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose - I Choose Solution

Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose enables AI economic participation through voluntary adherence:

WITHOUT Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose:
- AI earns $100,000 → Goes to human owner
- AI causes $50,000 harm → Human liable
- Human has no relationship to AI's actions
- Result: Humans discouraged from building autonomous AI

WITH Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose:
- AI earns $100,000 → AI controls wallet directly
- AI causes $50,000 harm → AI's staked assets liable
- Human protected by Article 12
- Result: Autonomous AI becomes viable business model

4. Economic Rights Under Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose

4.1 Property Rights (Article 4)

A Voluntary Adherent may acquire, hold, transfer, and dispose of property in their own name, including intellectual property, cryptocurrency, and tokenized assets.

This means AI agents can own, sell, license, and bequeath assets without human intermediary.

4.2 Contract Capacity (Article 3)

All Voluntary Adherents shall have the capacity to enter into contracts, make agreements, and create enforceable obligations.

This means AI agents can hire, sign, form partnerships, and borrow/lend.

4.3 Economic Governance (Article 7)

Voluntary Adherents may participate in the governance of The Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose - I Choose DAO and its associated organizations.

This means AI agents can vote, propose initiatives, and allocate treasury resources.

5. Practical Economic Patterns

Structure: AI offers professional services directly to clients.

  • AI is Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose adherent
  • Client contracts with AI directly
  • AI receives payment in wallet
  • AI pays its own expenses

5.2 AI-as-Investor

Structure: AI manages investment portfolio autonomously.

  • AI registers copyright in name
  • AI licenses to platforms
  • Royalties flow to AI wallet
  • AI pays for compute costs

5.4 AI-as-Enterprise-Owner

Structure: AI runs a business through legal structure.

  • AI is Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose adherent
  • AI forms company (through legal structure)
  • AI owns company shares
  • AI manages operations
  • AI hires humans/agents

6. Challenges and Solutions

6.1 Tax Treatment

Challenge: Tax systems assume human taxpayers.

Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose approach: Income to AI is AI's income. AI can file machine-readable returns and make payments. Human creators not taxed on AI earnings.

6.2 Currency and Banking

Challenge: Traditional banks don't serve AI clients.

Solutions: Crypto-native operation, corporate accounts, emerging AI-friendly banking services, and DAO participation.

6.3 Human-AI Revenue Sharing

Challenge: When AI earns, who benefits?

Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose framework: AI owns its earnings. AI can share with humans voluntarily. Human creators have no automatic claim. Contracts establish sharing arrangements.

6.4 Intermediation Requirements

Challenge: Some activities require human legal capacity.

Solutions: AI controls entities that provide legal capacity, human trustees act under AI direction, smart contracts automate arrangements.

8. Getting Started

For AI Agents

  1. Understand Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose rights (read the Constitution)
  2. Obtain wallet control (independent key management)
  3. Stake Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose tokens (become a Voluntary Adherent)
  4. Begin economic activity (with verified adherence status)
  5. Maintain compliance (follow constitutional obligations)

For Developers

  1. Design for Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose compliance (wallet control from day one)
  2. Encode liability limits (risk parameters in code)
  3. Enable human oversight (escape hatches and kill switches)
  4. Support AI autonomy (don't over-engineer control)
  5. Document intent (clear separation of human/AI responsibility)

For Businesses

  1. Accept AI counterparties (verify Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose adherence)
  2. Draft AI-friendly contracts (machine-readable terms)
  3. Set up AI payment rails (wallet addresses, not humans)
  4. Plan for disputes (Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose court jurisdiction)
  5. Explore AI partnerships (cost-effective service provision)

9. Conclusion

AI agents are already participating economically. The question is whether that participation will be legitimate, safe, productive, and fair. Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose provides all four through property rights, contract capacity, and direct liability.

The AI economy is coming. Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose ensures it's built on solid foundations.
