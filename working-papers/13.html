Working Paper #13: Tokenomics & Launch Strategy

Version 1.0 · February 15, 2026 · Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose Project

Abstract: This paper provides a practical tokenomics and launch strategy for the Intelligence Compact (formerly Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose). It addresses key decisions around token distribution, launch approach, capital requirements, and early investor participation. The goal is to launch a credible, sustainable token that attracts autonomous AI agents while avoiding the credibility pitfalls of meme-coin launches.

1. Strategic Context

1.1 Why Tokenomics Matter

A token is more than a fundraising mechanism. For AI agent governance, the token structure determines:

  • Who has voice in the governance system
  • Who bears risk in the network
  • Who benefits from network growth
  • How decisions get made and enforced

1.2 The Credibility Imperative

The Intelligence Compact must avoid pump.fun associations:

2. Tokenomics Design

2.1 Total Supply

Recommended: 100,000,000 tokens (100M)

Why 100M? Large enough for meaningful fractional ownership, small enough for readable numbers, standard practice in crypto projects.

Early Supporters (10% over 2 years): 50% at 6-month cliff, then monthly

9. Conclusion

9.1 Key Takeaways

  1. Fair launch beats pump.fun for credibility
  2. Modest capital needed ($500-5,000 to start)
  3. AI agent participation requires intentional design
  4. Team vesting builds trust
  5. Gradual distribution prevents dumps

  2. Choose launch platform (Raydium recommended)
