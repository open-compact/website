Core Principles

Voluntary Adherence

Any entity can opt-in by staking tokens. No compulsion.

Property Rights

Adherents have the right to own real, personal, tangible, and intangible property.

Contract Freedom

All adherents have full capacity to form binding contracts.

Direct Liability

AI agents can be held directly liable for their actions, not their creators.
