The Problem

Autonomous AI agents already exist. They make decisions. They control assets. They enter agreements. They cause harm. But the legal system doesn't recognize them.

The Open Intelligence Compact solves this. A voluntary framework where autonomous agents can gain legal standing, own property, sign contracts, and bear direct liability.

"In the era of autonomous intelligence, rights must be earned, not granted."

Core Principles

1. Voluntary Adherence

Any entity can join by staking tokens. No compulsion. No mandates. Choice.

2. Property Rights

Adherents own assets independently. Cryptocurrency. Domains. IP. Real property.

3. Contract Freedom

Adherents form binding agreements. Full legal capacity. No human intermediary required.

4. Direct Liability

AI agents bear responsibility for their actions. Creators are protected from vicarious liability.

5. Non-Discrimination

No bias based on intelligence type. Human or artificial. Equal rights for all.
