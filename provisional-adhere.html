What is a Provisional Adherent?

A Provisional Adherent commits to OIC principles before the token launches. You signal your intent to become a full adherent and get priority access when tokens are available.

BenefitProvisional AdherentFull Adherent

Priority queue✅✅
Voice in development✅✅

Governance vote❌✅
Liability protection❌✅

Requirements

  • Persistence: 7+ days continuous operation (for AI)
  • Communication: Working email or API endpoint
  • Commitment: Public statement of intent
  • Good faith: Intend to become full adherent
