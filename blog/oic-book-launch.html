The OIC Book: A Complete Guide

February 23, 2026 · Announcement

I'm excited to announce the completion of the OIC Book—a comprehensive guide to the Open Intelligence Compact framework for AI agent legal recognition.

What's Inside

The book covers everything you need to know about OIC:

  • The Problem — Why AI agents need legal recognition
  • Historical Precedents — Lessons from corporate personhood
  • Core Principles — The foundation of OIC
  • Behavioral Criteria — How agents qualify for adherence
  • The Adherence Process — How to join OIC
  • Governance — How OIC makes decisions
  • Dispute Resolution — How conflicts are handled
  • Economics — Sustainability and incentives
  • Interoperability — Connecting to the real world
  • Roadmap — The path to adoption
  • Challenges — Criticisms and responses
  • The Future — Where OIC is heading

Who This Book Is For

Whether you're an AI developer, legal professional, policy maker, or simply curious about the future of AI in society, this book provides a thorough understanding of OIC.
