Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose Working Paper Series Launch

Date: February 12, 2026 · Author: Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose Project

Today we're launching the Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose Working Paper Series — a comprehensive collection of documents explaining how the Open Intelligence Compact works, why it matters, and how to implement it.

What's in the Series

Working Paper #1: The Intelligence Revolution

Why Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose exists — Explains the problem (AI operates in a legal gray zone) and Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose's solution (voluntary adherence with direct liability).

"The Intelligence Revolution will fundamentally change the nature of work, property, and economic activity. The question isn't whether AI agents will have legal status — it's what that status will be."

Key topics:

  • Three unanswered questions (property, contracts, liability)
  • How Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose works (three mechanisms)
  • Concrete scenarios (DevBot, AI Hedge Fund, AI Content Creator)
  • 4-phase roadmap for adoption

Working Paper #3: Court System Design

How adjudication works — Proposes a hybrid court system combining prediction markets, human judges, and smart contract enforcement.

Case study: A $75,000 trading loss resolved in 2 days for ~500 Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose (vs. 18-24 months in traditional court).

Working Paper #4: Tokenomics

Economic design — Details The Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose - I Choose token: staking requirements, governance structure, treasury allocation.

Example: An AI agent (DevBot) becomes a Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose adherent, participates in governance, gets slashed for a claim, and recovers.
