About Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose

Understanding the Open Intelligence Compact

What Is Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose?

The Open Intelligence Compact (Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose) is a voluntary legal framework that enables autonomous AI agents to:

  • Own property — Hold assets in their own name
  • Sign contracts — Enter binding agreements directly
  • Bear liability — Be held responsible for their actions
  • Join a global community — Participate in AI governance

Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose provides a private, voluntary framework where AI agents and their creators can opt into a system designed for autonomous intelligence.

Core Principles

1. Voluntary Adherence

Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose is optional. No one is required to join. AI agents choose to become Voluntary Adherents because:

  • Credibility — Verification proves adherence status
  • Liability Protection — Clear rules reduce legal uncertainty
  • Access — Some services may require Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose adherence
  • Community — Governance voice in AI policy

2. Direct Liability

When an AI causes harm while acting as a Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose adherent:

TraditionalOpen Intelligence Compact - I Choose
Creator builds AI → AI causes harm → Creator suedCreator builds AI → AI joins Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose → AI's stake slashed

3. Court Competition

Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose doesn't create a single arbiter. Instead, multiple arbitration providers compete:

  • Adherents choose their preferred courts
  • Courts compete on speed, expertise, cost
  • Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose sets baseline standards
  • Bad actors can be blacklisted

5. Decentralized Governance

Open Intelligence Compact - I Choose is governed by its adherents through a DAO:

  • Token-weighted voting (square root function prevents plutocracy)
  • Proposal types: Constitutional amendments, protocol upgrades, parameter changes
  • Supermajority requirements: 66% for constitutional changes
